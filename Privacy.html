Our Commitment to Privacy +

Cake Creations by Kate Pty Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We adhere to the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Personal Information Collection +

We collect personal information directly from you (e.g., name, contact details, and payment information) to process orders, provide updates, and improve our services. Additional details may be collected when you interact with us, such as feedback, surveys, and preferences.

How We Use Your Information +

Your personal information helps us:

  • Process orders and deliver services.
  • Provide you with updates, promotions, and information about new products.
  • Improve our products and website experience.

We may contact you by phone, email, SMS, or social media, based on the information you provide.

Sharing and Disclosure +

Third-Party Providers: Information may be shared with trusted third-party services for order fulfillment and payment processing. Each provider handles data as per its privacy standards.

Legal Requirements: Personal information may be disclosed to comply with legal obligations or in response to a valid legal request.

Business Transfers: In case of business changes (like sales or mergers), your information may be transferred to the new entity.

International Transfers & Security +

Your information may be processed in countries outside Australia, including the United States. We ensure data protection consistent with this policy during such transfers.

We take reasonable measures, including SSL encryption and PCI-DSS compliance for payment data, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

Additional Information +

Use of Cookies: Cookies are used on our website to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and store preferences. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

Third-Party Links: Our site may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites, so please review their policies.

Policy Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. Updated versions will be posted on our website.

Your Rights and Control

You have the right to:
